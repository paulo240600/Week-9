Visit Piura!

Where Summer Never Ends

Majestic Beaches

This is undoubtedly one of Piura's greatest attractions. The beaches in this destination welcome thousands of visitors captivated not only by its warm climate but also by its waves that attract surfers from around the world. Among the most visited beaches are Mancora, Los Organos, Vichayito, and Cabo Blanco.

Exquisite dishes

Lastly, but not less important, is Piura's gastronomy. Notable dishes include Grouper Cebiche, Seco de cabrito (stewed goat), and Seco de chavelo. The latter is prepared with boiled and mashed green bananas, to which seasoned meat, tomato, Peruvian red pepper, and onion are added
